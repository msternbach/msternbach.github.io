My Data Journey

I’m Michael, I’m recently graduated MBA student looking for a full time job. I first became interested in data analytics/science last summer after taking a financial data analytics course. I didn’t know much about data analytics, but I enjoyed that class a lot so I decided to learn more about potential career paths. After reaching out to a few people and learning more about their careers I became set on getting a job in data analytics. Ever since then that interest has blossomed into a passion. Over the past few months I’ve spent countless hours learning new skills in python, SQL, and Tableau. Additionally, as someone who is very interested in A.I., my goal of getting a data analytics job has started to shift to getting a data scientist job. I'm actively looking for jobs now, and I'm excited to find out where I end up. No matter where it is I plan on bringing my newfound skills to the table and continue onto the next phase of my data journey.
