SQL Data Analysis of NFL Qbs Effectiveness Against Different Defensive Schemes

In this project I analyzed how NFL quarterbacks have performed against different defensive coverages over the past two years. My main findings were that certain coverages are run much more often than others, and quarterbacks do perform differently based on different defensive schemes. I provided the code, results, and analysis for each of the queries I ran. I encourage you to read the introduction as it provides information on some of the intricacies of the project, and it also provides a little background on the terminology for those that aren’t football fans.

TABLEAU VISUALIZATION OF NFL QBS EFFECTIVENESS AGAINST DIFFERENT DEFENSIVE SCHEMES

I visualized the SQL project with a dashboard containing 3 charts. The chart on the top of the dashboard compares the average stats of selected players accross different coverages. The one on the bottom left shows how often each coverage was called on average against each quarterback. The one on the bottom right shows individual player stats for every game over the past two seasons, and the percentile the selected coverage was run in those games. The dashboard is interactive. You can choose stat type, coverage type, and which players you want to see on the visualization. I encourage you to play around with the dashboard and look at it in fullscreen as it shows the best view.
